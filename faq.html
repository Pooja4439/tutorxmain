Course Enrollment

Can you please confirm if there is a free demo session available for every course? If so, could you provide details on how I can book a demo session?

Yes, TutorX provides free demo sessions for all courses. These sessions allow you to experience the tutors' teaching techniques firsthand and evaluate the course quality. To schedule your demo, simply visit our website, click on "Book Demo Session," fill out your details, and choose a suitable time for your demo.

What topics are covered in the demo session?

TutorX offers the flexibility to select specific topics tailored to your needs,.Additionally, you can schedule your demo session at your convenience, ensuring a comfortable and personalized learning experience.

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply click on any course and complete the registration form. The TutorX team will then reach out to you to discuss your further requirements and assist you in the next steps.

Do Indian tutors understand Australian accents and familiar with the Australian curriculum?

Indian tutors are well-versed in Australian accents and have a strong understanding of the Australian curriculum. They are trained to effectively communicate and adapt their teaching methods to ensure a smooth learning experience for students, making them capable of addressing diverse learning needs.

Is there a month-to-month payment option?

TutorX offers flexible pricing options based on your needs, charging on a per-session basis for courses. Additionally, we provide quarterly, half-yearly, and annual packages to accommodate various learning preferences and budgets.

How can I pay for the course?

TutorX offers a straightforward payment process through PayPal, Skibi, and credit cards. There is a platform fee of 6% included, but rest assured, there are no hidden charges.

How can I be sure that my money is secure when using this online platform?

You can trust that your money is secure with TutorX because we prioritize the safety and privacy of our users. Our platform employs advanced encryption technologies and secure payment gateways to protect your financial information. Additionally, we adhere to industry-standard security protocols to ensure a safe online experience. Feel free to reach out to our support team if you have any further concerns!
